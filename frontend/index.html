About EpiDetect

Engineering a clinical-grade architecture for advanced seizure detection using high-fidelity EEG signal processing and ensemble intelligence.

99.4% accuracy on the
Bonn EEG dataset.

10-fold cross-validation across 5 signal classes.

EpiDetect is a B.Tech final year project that applies ensemble machine learning to classify epileptic and non−epileptic EEG signals from the Bonn University dataset. The pipeline combines signal preprocessing, 720 hand−engineered features, and SHAP−based explainability to make predictions that are both accurate and interpretable.

Algorithm Refinement

To ensure high generalization, the ensemble uses multi-seed averaging across 5 unique seeds, mitigating variance and preventing overfitting on patient-specific patterns.

Advanced Selection

Feature selection is performed via a Union of Mutual Information (MI) and F-classif ANOVA scores, prioritizing features with high discriminative power for ictal states.
